What is MedTrack

MedTrack is the office system that replaces manual paper steps in a medical testing facility. It brings scheduling, tracking, and results into one secure portal so every step is simple and clear.

Think of it as your lab operations hub. It organizes orders, follows each vial through testing, and delivers results to the right people.

Who is it for

  • Office staff use MedTrack to schedule patients, submit or view orders, and answer questions fast.
  • Doctors can schedule patients, review order logs, and see results as soon as they are ready.
  • Patients can check the status of their tests and view their full lab reports online when the office shares them.

How does it work

  1. Schedule the patient in the portal or request a pickup through the appointment page.
  2. Label vials and submit the order. Barcodes or tags are scanned at intake for accurate tracking.
  3. Each station updates status in real time. Staff and doctors can see progress at a glance.
  4. Results are posted to the portal and can flow into your EMR or EHR if you choose.
  5. Patients can sign in to see their full report online and can leave a review of their experience.

For larger teams, MedTrack can grow with options like RFID tracking, AI powered queue estimates, and EMR or EHR connections. See the tiers page for details.

Why is it needed

Many labs still rely on handwritten notes and separate spreadsheets. That slows things down and leads to errors. MedTrack gives you one clear source of truth.

  • Faster scheduling and fewer phone calls
  • Accurate tracking from intake to final report
  • Fewer errors from lost notes or double entry
  • Simple access to results and full reports for staff, doctors, and patients
  • Easy reporting and export for audits and quality checks
