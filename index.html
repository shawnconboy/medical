Trusted medical testing, fast results

American Labs provides comprehensive testing and quick secure reporting to medical offices across the Midwest. MedTrack keeps technicians and clients informed at every step from sample intake to final report delivery.

Services

  • Blood chemistry and hematology testing
  • Microbiology and culture testing
  • Pathology and molecular diagnostics
  • Rapid result delivery via secure web portal
  • Barcode and RFID tracking and inventory management
  • EMR and EHR integrations Epic, Cerner, Athena Health

How MedTrack works

  1. Clients submit sample vials with printed barcodes or place orders through the portal.
  2. Vials are scanned at intake and tracked through testing stations.
  3. Technicians update status at each step and clients see real time progress online.
  4. Final results are delivered securely and can be routed into the client's EMR or EHR.
